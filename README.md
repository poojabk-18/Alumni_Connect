Alumni Connect
Overview

Alumni Connect is a networking platform designed to bridge the gap between students and alumni within a specific college ecosystem.
It empowers students to connect with graduates for mentorship, career guidance, and professional opportunities while enabling alumni to give back to their institution and community.

The platform addresses a common challenge faced by students — limited access to experienced professionals who can guide them on the skills, technologies, and career paths needed to succeed in today's competitive job market.

Unlike generic networking platforms, Alumni Connect is designed to be deployed as a service for individual colleges, giving institutions administrative control over their own alumni-student network.

Problem Statement

Students often face:

Limited access to alumni networks
Lack of mentorship and career guidance
Difficulty identifying which technologies or skills to learn
Weak professional connections within their institution

Alumni, on the other hand, may:

Want to contribute back to their college
Offer mentorship or workshops
Provide project opportunities or client referrals
Build professional networks with students

Colleges also need:

A centralized alumni engagement platform
Better visibility into student-alumni collaboration
Tools to manage mentorship and networking activities
Solution

Alumni Connect provides a structured digital platform where:

Students can discover and connect with alumni
Alumni can mentor students and conduct workshops
Colleges can manage and monitor alumni engagement
Alumni can refer talented students to clients or opportunities
A strong professional ecosystem can be built within the institution

The platform is designed to function as a Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) solution for colleges.
